Why this exists

Kolkata moves by bus, but the route data is scattered. People rely on memory, local advice, and half-complete route lists to figure out how to get across the city. I built this as a small attempt to make that fragmented knowledge searchable: normalize the stop names, connect the routes, and let anyone see possible journeys in one place.
